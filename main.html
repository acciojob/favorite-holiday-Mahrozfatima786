Paris, France


Paris, the City of Light, is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. Known for its romantic atmosphere, world-class art, rich history, and delicious cuisine, Paris offers an unforgettable experience for travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous painting Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – One of the most beautiful avenues in the world.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A romantic way to see the city’s monuments.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Buttery and flaky breakfast pastry.
  • Baguette – A classic French bread that’s a staple in every meal.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – Colorful French sweets with a crispy shell.
  • Escargot – A traditional dish made with snails and garlic butter.
