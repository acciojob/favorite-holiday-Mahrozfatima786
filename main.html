Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light". It’s a world-famous destination renowned for its rich history, iconic landmarks, romantic ambiance, and exceptional cuisine.

  1. A symbol of Paris and a must-see for every visitor.
  2. Home to the Mona Lisa and countless other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral A gothic architectural marvel located on the Île de la Cité.
  4. Champs-Élysées– One of the most famous avenues in the world, known for shopping and entertainment.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica– A beautiful white-domed church offering panoramic views of the city.
  • Croissants– Flaky, buttery pastries that are perfect for breakfast.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with a variety of sweet or savory ingredients.
  • Escargot– A traditional delicacy of cooked land snails.
  • Ratatouille– A hearty vegetable stew from Provence, often served in Paris.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate cookies with sweet fillings.
