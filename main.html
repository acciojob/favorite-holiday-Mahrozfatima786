Paris, France


Paris, also known as the City of Light, is one of the most beautiful and romantic destinations in the world. It is famous for its art, culture, cuisine, and stunning architecture.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – An iconic symbol of Paris and a must-visit landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – The most famous avenue in Paris, perfect for shopping and strolling.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A magical way to see Paris by night with illuminated landmarks.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries that are perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate French sweets.
  • Escargots – Snails cooked with garlic and butter, a true French delicacy.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be enjoyed sweet or savory.
  • French Cheese – From creamy Brie to strong Roquefort, a cheese lover’s paradise.
