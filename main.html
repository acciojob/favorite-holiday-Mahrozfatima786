Switzerland


Switzerland is a beautiful country in Europe known for its Alps, lakes, and picturesque villages. It offers a perfect blend of nature, adventure, and culture.

  1. Mount Matterhorn – iconic mountain peak
  2. Lake Geneva – stunning lakeside views
  3. Jungfraujoch – the “Top of Europe”
  4. Château de Chillon – historic castle by the lake
  5. Swiss Alps – paradise for skiing and hiking
  • Fondue – melted cheese served with bread
  • Rösti – crispy potato dish
  • Swiss Chocolate – world-renowned delicacy
  • Älplermagronen – Alpine pasta with cheese and potatoes
  • Basler Läckerli – spiced gingerbread from Basel
