Switzerland


Switzerland is a beautiful country in Europe known for its Alps, lakes, and villages.

  1. Mount Matterhorn – iconic mountain peak
  2. Lake Geneva – beautiful lakeside views
  3. Jungfraujoch – the “Top of Europe”
  4. Château de Chillon – historic castle
  5. Swiss Alps – skiing and hiking paradise
  • Fondue – melted cheese with bread
  • Rösti – crispy potato dish
  • Swiss Chocolate – world famous
  • Älplermagronen – Alpine pasta with cheese
  • Basler Läckerli – spiced gingerbread
